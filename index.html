Motorcycling

Getting Started

Introduction

The hobby of motorcycling consists largely of spending money so that you can do most of the same things that you can do in a car but be much less safe about it. Don't misunderstand. It is exhilarating and fun. It just seems a bit silly when evaluated objectively. The first thing you should do, even before getting a motorcycle is to get a helmet and some leather gloves. Next you should take a basic motorcycle rider class. This really is the safest way to get started. Once you pass the class and have a motorcycle endorsement on your driver's license. Then you can look into buying a motorcycle.
